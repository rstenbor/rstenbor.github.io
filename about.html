About Me

Greetings! I’m Ryan - I’m relatively new on the design scene and I’m raring to go. I discovered my passion for design a few years back when I worked for a small ESD testing lab. Part of my duties at the lab (outside of maintaining normal business operations) was to build and design a website. After taking a crash course in all things HTML and CSS, I found my calling in web design. I have spent a ton of time as a student in multiple fields, from electrical engineering, fine art, architecture, geology, and more. I recently graduated from the Graphic Information Technology program at ASU. This wide breadth of knowledge has led me to a deeper understanding of the world around me and a greater drive to further improve it!
