Bergen Piano Studio

The goal of this project was to display an understanding of JavaScript. I had to present two of three different modules. For the modules, I chose a "product" section, which displays a product than can be switched out for another, and a "shop" section, which allows users to add items to a shopping cart. I also had to have a validated form with built-in error messages, and a dark mode switch to change the theme of the website.

Outline & Process

I used CORSAIR as heavy inspiration for style and structure of this site, and all the product images were taken directly from CORSAIR.

Since I had my inspiration chosen, I drew up some wireframes in Adobe XD to get my structure well defined. I didn't end up doing a full mockup after wireframing, because I was just applying the visual style of CORSAIR to a slightly different structure.

The product section displays the first of three products, and a user can switch between all three. I used JavaScript to set the display of each content block to either "block" or "hidden" depending on whether or not it should be visible.

The shop section displays a bunch of different CORSAIR products, along with price information and a button to "Add to Order". At the bottom, the products added are displayed in a list, and the total cost, and cost after taxes, is displayed. Clicking "ORDER" clears the list and price values and alerts the client of the total.

The form section is rather simple in concept but required knowledge of REGEX expressions and other validation tactics. The user can choose between inputting a phone or email address. When "Submit" is clicked, the form checks that a name, a valid phone number or email address, and comments have been filled in. If all the conditions pass, the form alerts the user letting them know their info went through.
